About Us

Aqil & Aqil Advocates is a renowned Lahore-based law firm established in 1961

Leading in Legal Services for 64 Years

Aqil & Aqil Advocates was founded in 1961 by Mr. Justice (Retd.) Aqil Mirza, and since then we have established ourselves as a trusted and respected law firm in Lahore. Our mission is to provide our clients with the best legal consultation and representation in their legal matters.

Our firm has been prominent in the development of law and providing integrated services to clients. We are experts in litigating cutting-edge issues, counseling clients, and negotiating & drafting contracts to deal with novel situations.
